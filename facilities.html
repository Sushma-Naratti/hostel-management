Facilities Provided By Our Hostel

Accommodation Options:


Welcome to our vibrant hostel, where we've redefined shared living to make it an experience that's both comfortable and exciting. Choose from our array of rooms, whether you prefer the camaraderie of 2, 3, or 4 sharing configurations. Our rooms come with the added perk of shared bathroom and washroom facilities that are not just clean but also shareable – fostering a sense of community among residents. Here, we've made your comfort a priority. Imagine stepping into a bathroom where you have the choice of hot or cold water whenever you desire, thanks to our solar and geyser facilities. It's like having a spa-like experience right in your own space! Worried about cleanliness? Fret not! Our diligent team of servants and helpers ensures that your living quarters are sparkling clean and well-maintained. Each room is a haven designed for your convenience – featuring two fans to keep things cool, two lights for the perfect ambiance, and a window for a breath of fresh air. We believe in providing not just a room but a living experience that embraces comfort, community, and a touch of luxury. Welcome to a hostel where your living space is as exciting as your academic journey

Mess Facilities and Meal Times:


At our hostel, we understand the importance of nourishing both mind and body. That's why we take pride in offering top-notch kitchen and dining facilities that cater to the diverse tastes and dietary preferences of our residents. Our spacious and well-equipped kitchen provides a hub for culinary creativity, where residents can unleash their inner chefs and prepare delicious meals together. Whether you're whipping up a quick snack or preparing a feast, you'll find everything you need right at your fingertips. In our welcoming mess hall, residents can enjoy wholesome and nutritious meals prepared by our talented chefs. Our breakfast service runs from 8:00 am to 10:30 am, ensuring a hearty start to the day for early risers and late sleepers alike. Lunch is served from 12:30 pm to 3:00 pm, offering a midday reprieve for residents to refuel and recharge. For those in need of a pick-me-up, snacks and tea are available in the evening from 5:00 pm to 6:00 pm. Finally, dinner service begins at 7:30 pm and continues until 9:00 pm, providing a satisfying end to the day with a variety of delectable dishes to choose from. At our hostel, we believe that good food brings people together, and we're committed to providing an exceptional dining experience that nourishes both body and soul at every mealtime.

Connectivity & Convenience:


At our hostel, we understand the importance of staying connected in today's digital world. That's why we provide unlimited Wi-Fi access to all our residents, ensuring that they can stay connected anytime, anywhere. Whether it's for academic research, staying in touch with loved ones, or simply browsing the web for entertainment, our high-speed internet ensures seamless connectivity for all. Additionally, we recognize the need for uninterrupted power supply, which is why we guarantee 24-hour electricity throughout our premises. Residents can rest assured knowing that they can study, work, or relax without any disruptions, thanks to our reliable power infrastructure. At our hostel, we're committed to providing a comfortable and convenient living environment where residents can thrive both online and offline.

Hygiene and Comfort: Water and Washroom Facilities:


At our hostel, we prioritize the well-being and comfort of our residents by ensuring access to clean and refreshing water around the clock. Our state-of-the-art facilities guarantee 24-hour availability of both clean drinking water and hot bathing water, providing convenience and peace of mind to all our residents. We understand the importance of maintaining hygiene standards, which is why our water tanks undergo regular cleaning to ensure the purity and safety of the water supply. Additionally, in our hostel, both general and separate washroom/bathroom facilities are available, meticulously cleaned and maintained to uphold the highest standards of hygiene. During the summer months, residents can enjoy the added comfort of cold water availability to beat the heat. With our commitment to providing reliable and refreshing water services, as well as pristine bathroom and washroom facilities, residents can rest assured knowing their hydration, bathing, and hygiene needs are well taken care of at all times.

Security and Surveillance:


At our hostel, we prioritize the safety and security of our residents above all else. That's why our premises are equipped with state-of-the-art CCTV surveillance systems, ensuring round-the-clock monitoring and oversight. With strategically placed cameras covering key areas throughout the hostel, including entrances, corridors, and common areas, we maintain a vigilant watch to deter any potential security threats. The presence of CCTV surveillance not only acts as a deterrent to unauthorized access but also provides residents with peace of mind knowing that their safety is our top priority. Our commitment to ensuring a secure living environment extends beyond the physical infrastructure – it's a reflection of our dedication to fostering a community where residents can feel safe, comfortable, and at home.
