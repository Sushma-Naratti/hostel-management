Pricing Details
At our hostel, we believe in providing affordable accommodation options that cater to the diverse needs of our residents. For our 2 sharing rooms, the fee is set at 30,900 rupees per academic year, with an additional deposit of 5,000 rupees required upon admission. This deposit is refundable upon completion of the academic year or upon withdrawal, minus any outstanding fees or deductions for damages. Similarly, for our 3 and 4 sharing rooms, the fee is 35,900 rupees per academic year, with the same refundable deposit requirement for freshmen. However, for subsequent years, there is no need to pay the deposit, only the actual accommodation fees. We strive to make our hostel accessible to all students while ensuring a fair and transparent fee structure.
